A collection of miscellaneous vanity items, focused on either unique effects or tiny accessories.
Has support for [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2878558240]Asymmetric Equips[/url].

Content Summary:
[list][*]A set of items that allow the player to dye each part of their base player, including their skin, eyes, eye whites, and default clothing.
[*]A hair dye that takes on the effects of any dye used on it.
[*]A set of contacts which can force the player's eyes open, half-closed, or closed. They can also force the opposite and never let the player's eyes reach a state.
[*]A harness that allows you to hide your arms.
[*]A generic bracelet.
[*]You can now use paints as dyes.
[*]You can now wear some birds on your shoulders.[/list]

All items in the mod can be purchased from various NPCs -- see the Discussions tab for specifics.
